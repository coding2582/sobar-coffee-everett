Our Story

A small shop with a soft spot for Everett

"Downtown deserved a shop that takes its coffee seriously without taking itself too seriously."

Sobar opened on Colby Avenue with one idea in mind. We make our syrups in small batches behind the counter, dial in the espresso every morning, and learn our regulars' names faster than they expect.

On any given day you'll find students at the big table, nurses coming off shift, and at least one person who swears they only came for the banana bread. Come as you are. We'll have the kettle on.

Beans we stand behind

Roasted in the Pacific Northwest and dialed in fresh every morning.

Neighbors first

Locally owned and staffed by people who live here too.

Made to order

House syrups and real ingredients, even where nobody would notice.
